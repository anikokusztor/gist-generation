Hello! Thank you for your interest in our study.

We are interested to find out your general impression on some images that reflect day-to-day lives. You will see each image briefly (less than half a second), then we will ask you to provide five words to describe your impression, and how confidence you are for each of the word.

The experiment will take approximately 30 minutes of your time. Don't worry! Further instructions will be provided before the experiment, and you get three practice trials.

All data will be kept confidential. If you have any question at this stage, please click on any of the email links above to contact the investigators.
